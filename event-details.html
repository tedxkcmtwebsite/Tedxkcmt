Why Should You Attend a TEDx Event?

TEDx events are a gateway to exploring new ideas and expanding your horizons. It’s an opportunity to listen to speakers who challenge conventional thinking and inspire innovation. Beyond the talks, TEDx events bring together a community of curious minds, fostering meaningful conversations and connections. It’s not just an event; it’s an experience that leaves you motivated and ready to take on the world with fresh perspectives.

TED

TED, a nonprofit dedicated to "ideas worth spreading," shares transformative insights through short, impactful talks on Technology, Entertainment, and Design. With over 3 billion annual viewers, TED connects global audiences to innovators and experts, inspiring change and fostering knowledge that matters.

TEDx

TEDx, inspired by TED's mission of sharing "ideas worth spreading," brings transformative ideas to local communities. It encourages students to think beyond textbooks, fostering curiosity, innovation, and open-minded learning.

TEDxKCMT

TEDxKCMT, hosted by Kristu Jyoti College of Management and Technology, is Kerala's first institution to host TED Circles. This annual event showcases groundbreaking ideas from diverse speakers, focusing on science, technology, arts, culture, and social issues. Combining TEDTalks, live speakers, workshops, and networking, it inspires innovation, fosters community, and sparks meaningful discussions. An unmissable experience for those seeking creativity and collaboration!
